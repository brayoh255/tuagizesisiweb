Our Tech Journey

From startup to Tanzania's leading technology solutions provider

Driving Digital Transformation

Founded in 2020, TUAGIZE SISI began as a small tech startup with just two developers in Dar es Salaam. Our founders recognized the growing need for customized digital solutions that could support Tanzania's rapidly evolving business landscape.

What started as a modest operation has grown into a nationwide technology partner serving hundreds of businesses. Today, we're proud to be Tanzania's fastest-growing tech company, combining cutting-edge innovation with local expertise to provide unmatched service quality.

Our team of over 200 dedicated technology professionals works around the clock to ensure your digital needs are met. We've invested heavily in research, infrastructure, and training to build a tech company you can trust.
